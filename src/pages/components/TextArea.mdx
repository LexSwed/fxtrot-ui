## TextArea

`TextField`-like element but `textarea` and with automatic size adjustment.

```tsx preview
<Row gap="md">
  <TextArea label="Enter your name" placeholder="John Doe" />
  <TextArea label="Enter your name" placeholder="John Doe" secondaryLabel="(disabled)" disabled />
  <TextArea label="Enter your name" placeholder="John Doe" secondaryLabel="(read only)" readOnly />
</Row>
```

## Variants

```tsx preview
<Row gap="lg">
  <Column gap="md">
    <TextArea label="Boxed" hint="Default variant" placeholder="Default boxed variant" />
    <TextArea variant="underlined" hint="Pretty variant" label="Underlined" placeholder="Pretty underlined" />
    <TextArea variant="flat" label="Flat variant" hint="Press Esc to cancel" placeholder="Flat variant" />
  </Column>
  <Column gap="md">
    <TextArea
      label="Boxed"
      secondaryLabel="(disabled)"
      hint="Default variant"
      placeholder="Default boxed variant"
      disabled
    />
    <TextArea
      variant="underlined"
      hint="Pretty variant"
      secondaryLabel="(disabled)"
      label="Underlined"
      placeholder="Pretty underlined"
      disabled
    />
    <TextArea disabled variant="flat" label="Flat variant" hint="Press Esc to cancel" placeholder="Flat variant" />
  </Column>
</Row>
```

## TextLink

`Text` component as `anchor`. Enables additional props, such as `external` to ease the use and security of `target="_blank"`

```tsx preview
<Column gap="2" cross="start">
  <TextLink href="https://random.dog/">Inline, within text</TextLink>
  <TextLink href="https://random.dog/" inline={false}>
    Just a link, as a text block or a wrapper around other elements
  </TextLink>
</Column>
```

### External

```tsx preview
<Column gap="8" cross="start" css={{ width: '50%' }}>
  <TextLink href="https://random.dog/" external>
    Just external
  </TextLink>
  <TextLink href="https://random.dog/" external="icon">
    External with icon
  </TextLink>
  <p>
    Within text:
    <br /> The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy <TextLink href="https://random.dog/" external="icon">
      dog
    </TextLink>, but can't make it, or maybe can but we just don't know. And here's a wrapped link:
    <TextLink href="https://random.dog/" external>
      Is it really a long link?
    </TextLink>
  </p>
</Column>
```
